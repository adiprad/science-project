Matrix

The objective of the "Matrix" puzzle is to select the light-green squares in the grid.

  • When you click the "Start" button, you will be shown a 5x5 grid of light-blue squares.
  • 5 of the squares will be light-green in color. Memorize these squares!
  • After 4 seconds the squares will become light-blue again.
  • Select the squares that turned light-green.
  • You will have 30 seconds to finish it.
  • When you are finished, click “Submit”.
  • Good Luck!

Letter

The objective of the “Letter” puzzle is to memorize a random set of letters and type in the letter that is asked for.

  • When you click the “Start” button, you will see a random set of 8 letters. Memorize these!
  • After 7 seconds, you will be asked to type in one of the letters shown to you.
  • You will have 20 seconds to finish it.
  • When you are finished, click “Submit”.
  • Good Luck!

Concentration

The objective of the "Concentration" puzzle is to identify the matching tiles.

  • When you click the “Start” button, you will see a 4x4 grid of black tiles.
  • When you click on a tile, it will show it’s color.
  • Select 2 tiles of the same color to solve the pair.
  • Solve all eight pairs to complete the task.
  • You will have 60 seconds to finish it.
  • When you are finished, click “Submit”.
  • Good Luck!
